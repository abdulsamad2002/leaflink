#  Leaflink


> One Link, Infinite Possibilities

A powerful link management platform that allows you to create a personalized landing page for all your social media profiles, websites, and important links. Share one simple URL and give your audience access to everything you offer.

##  Features

- **Simple & Fast** - Create your link page in seconds. No technical skills required.
- **Beautiful Design** - Modern, responsive design that looks great on any device.
- **Secure & Reliable** - Built with modern security practices to keep your data safe.
- **Always Free** - Create unlimited links and share them with the world, completely free.

##  How It Works

1. **Sign Up** - Create your free account using Google sign-in. Quick and secure.
2. **Add Your Links** - Add all your social media, websites, and important links to your dashboard.
3. **Share Your Page** - Get your unique Leaflink URL and share it everywhere. One link to rule them all!

##  Tech Stack

- Next.js
- NextAuth.js (Google OAuth)
- React Hook Form
- Tailwind CSS
